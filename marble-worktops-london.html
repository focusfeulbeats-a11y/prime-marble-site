Why choose marble for your worktop

Marble brings a natural depth of veining and a soft, luxurious finish no man-made surface fully replicates. It's a statement material — and with the right sealing and care, it performs beautifully in a real family kitchen.

Natural veining

No two slabs are alike. We help you choose and match veining flow across joins so the worktop reads as one continuous piece.

Precision templating

We template on site to the millimetre — accounting for appliances, sinks and hob cut-outs before a single cut is made.

Clean, tight seams

Careful edge preparation and colour-matched resin keep joins as close to invisible as physically possible.

Popular marble types

A few of the marbles we install most often in London kitchens.

Carrara

Soft grey veining on a white-grey base. A timeless, versatile classic that suits most kitchen styles.

Calacatta

Bolder, more dramatic gold or grey veining on a brighter white base — a true statement worktop.

Statuario

Bright white background with striking, sculptural veining. A premium choice for feature islands.

FAQs

How long does installation take?

A standard kitchen worktop is usually fitted in a day once templating is complete. Larger or more complex layouts may take longer.

Does marble need sealing?

Yes — we seal every marble worktop on installation, and recommend re-sealing periodically to protect against staining.

Can you match an existing slab?

Where possible, yes. Send photos of your current worktop or inspiration images and we'll advise on the closest match.

Do you remove the old worktop?

We can arrange removal and disposal of your existing worktop as part of the job — just let us know when you enquire.
